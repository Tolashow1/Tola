About Us

Welcome to Eat Better, join us on our journey to provide nutritious and organic ready made meals to low income families and across America. Eat better is an Organization that values and updates our costumers. Have a look through our website to find background information, latest products and how to contact us!

History

"Eat Better" was created in 2020 by Tola Showunmi and Mark Hughes . Reflection during covid 19 pandemic led our team to want to find a way to get cheap healthy food to as many children as possible across America .The main aim of the company was to provide a solution to the food industry is it possible to have cheap, healthy, and organic ready to go meals. Our goal is to be able to get our meals in low-income areas and schools across USA

Why use us

Our company is solving a problem that the rest of the food industry has avoided , which is selling quality food for affordable prices .We as a company believe all children deserve access to quality food and due to our affordable prices , more children across America will have this access.
